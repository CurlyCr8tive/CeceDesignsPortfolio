Health Equity NYC - Portfolio Summary

Project Overview

Health Equity NYC is a community-focused data visualization and analytics dashboard that empowers NYC residents, health workers, and advocates to understand health disparities across the five boroughs. The application combines real-world health data with environmental factors to tell the story of health equity in New York City in plain, accessible language.

**Live Demo:** [vercel.com/cherice-herons-projects](https://vercel.com/cherice-herons-projects/v0-health-equity-nyc-8b)

1. Advanced Multi-Dimensional Filtering**

3. Dynamic Data Visualizations

- Health condition prevalence charts by borough
- 5-year trend analysis

- Responsive, mobile-friendly charts


4. Plain-Language Community Insights

- "X out of 100 neighbors affected" metrics
- Community health status assessments (Good / Concerning / Needs Attention)

- Simple explanations of what data means for communities

5. AI-Powered Report Generation

- OpenAI integration for automated community health reports
- Personalized area assessments with comparisons to NYC averages

- CSV export for data analysis


6. Educational Resources

- Health Condition Library: 6+ major conditions with NYC-specific data

- Community Action Guides: How to advocate for improvements

7. Data Export & Sharing

- CSV export with community context
